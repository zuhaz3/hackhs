FAQ


What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a place where people write code for 24 hours in order to create something awesome. Hackers are given free food and drinks and also get to compete for amazing prizes.

What should I bring?

A laptop for coding and anything you need to code with. We'll provide the food and snacks to keep you energized.

Who is eligible to come?

Any student currently enrolled in High School or below. Sorry college students, we can't let you in due to space limitations and competitive aspects.



What can I build?

You are free to build anything you can want at this hackathon. In addition, usage of our sponsor APIs makes you eligible for sponsor prizes.

How big are teams?

Teams can be as big as you want, but we reconmend a team cap of 4 people. Keep in mind that we are not responsible for splitting prizes with your team.

Is this event actually free?

Yes! Thanks to our generous sponsors, we are able to provide this event free of charge. Our goal is to bring as many high school students into technology as possible.



Can I work on previous projects?

We request that you build whatever you wish to present at the hackathon. We do allow you to build on top of your old code such as adding in a new feature. Just be sure to ask us and clarify that when presenting.

I don't live close to this event. Can I come?

You sure can, just keep in mind that at this time, we are unable to offer travel reimbursements for students. This may change as we get closer to the event, so keep a eye on this page!

Are parents allowed to come?

We're still trying to figure that out right now :)
